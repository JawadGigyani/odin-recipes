White Handi

INGREDIENTS

  • Chicken Boneless, Cubes : 1/2 KG
  • Ginger Paste : 1 Tablespoon
  • Garlic Paste : 1 Tablespoon
  • Salt : To Taste
  • Yogurt : 1 Cup
  • Cream : ½ Cup
  • Green Chili, Chopped : For Garnish
  • Fresh Coriander,Chopped : For Garnish

STEPS

  1. Heat oil over medium flame in a pot. Add ginger and garlic paste. Sauté.
  2. Add cubed chicken and stir fry until no longer pink.
  3. Now add salt and black pepper, and mix.
  4. Stir in yogurt and simmer for 8 minutes.
  5. Add cream and mix well.
  6. Garnish chopped coriander and green chilies.
  7. Enjoy! Please give us your feedback in the comments below.
